Government Sponsored Schemes

In today's trying times, every business is struggling for its survival. To boost the SMEs, Governments all over the world have implemented several measures. The list of such schemes and initiatives is quite extensive which showcases the sector's importance and why the Governments are keen on helping them grow. The idea is to create an environment so that they remain sustainable and competitive.

The key benefits of government startup schemes are as follows -

  • Financial assistance
  • Facilitate funding
  • Provide collateral-free loans
  • Offer mentorship
  • Tax exemptions
  • Networking opportunities
  • Easy access to vital resources
  • Loans with low-interest rates
  • Relief funds
  • Income-based tax credits
  • Provide technical assistance
  • Access to a large market
  • Relaxed regulations
  • Government tenders
